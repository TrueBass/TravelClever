## About The Project

[![Product Name Screen Shot][product-screenshot]](https://example.com)

Designed to redefine the way you manage expenses with friends on your journeys! Say goodbye to the hassle of keeping track of exchange rates and who owes what during your travels with our user-friendly platform.

Here's why:
* Instant Currency Conversion via Camera
* Split bills in multiple currencies with no headaches
* Intuitive and transparent share costs for meals, accommodation, or activities
* Allows to pay a loan back in chosen currency

Snatch this app pronto and plunge headfirst into joyrides that'll have you laughing all the way to the bank! Smokin'!
